VPIP: (Voluntarility Put in Pot)
	-% of hands in which player voluntarility put money into pot

PFR: (Preflop Raise)
	-% of hands which player makes a preflop raise

AFq: (Agression Frequency)
	-% of bets,raises vs bets,raises,calls,folds
